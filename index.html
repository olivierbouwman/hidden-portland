Hidden Portland Map

This map lets you explore the "hidden" Portland. Use it to discover little treasures in your neighborhood, or to see how things around you looked in the past when you're out and about.

Most markers on this map are from the Hidden Portland for the Curious community. A wonderful group of people who find and share the creative, weird, and wonderful things they found around town.

The map also includes markers with historical photos from the Portland Archives.

I hope this map and the wonderful content provided by countless people within it brings a little joy to your day. If you like it, please feel free to share it with others.

       

Note: about a quarter of the markers are in the wrong spot due to the technical difficulty of automatically finding the correct location from a piece of text. If you feel so inclined, you can fix a marker using the "Fix location" link in each marker popup.

Code for this map is available on GitHub.
